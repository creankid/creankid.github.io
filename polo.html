Polos are required at school everyday, even under jackets. The most common color is navy blue, with a close second being gray. White isn't really a popular color because it tends to get dirty really fast and it is see through in most lighting. For girls, I would personally recommend getting the women's cut over the unisex because it makes you look less like a box and the sleeves are a better length.
